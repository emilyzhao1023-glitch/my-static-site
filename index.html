How ordering works

A simple, repeatable process for bulk orders.

1) Send inquiry
Style, quantity, logo file, target market, delivery country.
2) Quote + mockup
We confirm materials, decoration, and provide a clear timeline.
3) Sampling
Pre-production sample for approval (photos + shipping if needed).
4) Bulk + QC
Production, inspection, packing photos, then ship by air/sea/express.
